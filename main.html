The story follows the powerful villain Thanos, who wants to collect all six Infinity Stones. His goal is to wipe out half of all life in the universe to restore balance. The Avengers and other heroes like Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, and the Guardians of the Galaxy try to stop him. They fight battles in different parts of the universe to protect the stones. However, Thanos successfully collects all the stones and snaps his fingers, causing half of the universe’s population, including many heroes, to disappear. Ending: The movie ends with the heroes defeated and the universe in chaos, setting up the events of Avengers: Endgame.
